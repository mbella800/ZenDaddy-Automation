This guide explains how to use the script, navigate the OLED menu, and what
every setting does so you know exactly what to change.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   PART 1: CONTROLLER SHORTCUTS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. TURN ON SCRIPT
   Hold [R1] and press [DPAD UP]
   LED turns Green = Script is Active

2. TURN OFF SCRIPT
   Hold [R1] and press [DPAD DOWN]
   LED turns Red = Script is Disabled (normal controller)

3. OPEN SETTINGS MENU
   Hold [L2] and press [OPTIONS]
   The OLED screen switches to the settings menu

4. CLOSE SETTINGS MENU
   Hold [L2] and press [OPTIONS] again
   Returns to the main HUD screen, settings are saved automatically

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   PART 2: MENU NAVIGATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The menu has 5 pages. Each page has 2-3 settings you can adjust.

   UP / DOWN .......... Select which setting to change (arrow shows selection)
   LEFT / RIGHT ....... Lower / Raise the selected value
   L1 ................. Previous page
   R1 ................. Next page
   TRIANGLE ........... Reset ALL settings to factory defaults

The bottom of the screen shows 5 dots - the filled dot shows which page
you are on. A progress bar shows where your current value sits in its range.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   PART 3: SETTINGS GUIDE - WHAT EACH SETTING DOES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

=== PAGE 1: SCAN ===
Controls the rotational aim assist engine that activates Fortnite's tracking.

ROT - Rotation Strength (Range: 5 - 40, Default: 19)
   How strong the rotational pull is. This is the main "magnet feel" setting.
   Higher = stronger pull towards enemies.
   Lower  = subtler, more natural aim movement.
   START HERE: If aim assist feels too weak, raise this first.
   WARNING: Above 30 can cause visible screen movement.

HLX - Helix Pattern (Range: 4 - 24, Default: 12)
   Controls the circular movement pattern that triggers Fortnite's aim assist.
   Higher = wider circle = activates aim assist at greater range from target.
   Lower  = tighter circle = more precise but shorter range activation.
   LEAVE AT DEFAULT unless you know what you are doing.

SCN - Scan Speed (Range: 1 - 5, Default: 2)
   How fast the aim assist engine ramps up to full strength.
   1 = Very slow ramp (smooth but slow to kick in)
   5 = Instant full strength (aggressive, may feel jerky)
   RECOMMENDED: 2-3 for most players.

=== PAGE 2: MAGNET ===
Controls the magnetic stick effect - how your aim "locks" onto targets.

MAG - Magnet Strength (Range: 0 - 30, Default: 7)
   Extra pull force towards enemies when aim assist detects a target.
   0  = No extra magnet (rely only on Fortnite's native assist)
   30 = Maximum pull (very strong lock-on, can feel unnatural)
   SWEET SPOT: 5-10 for balanced play.

MSP - Magnet Speed (Range: 5 - 30, Default: 15)
   How quickly the magnet effect kicks in after detecting a target.
   5  = Very fast snap (instant lock)
   30 = Slow gradual pull (smoother tracking)
   LOWER = more snappy, better for flick shots.
   HIGHER = more gradual, better for tracking moving targets.

=== PAGE 3: SPEED ===
Controls sensitivity dampening - how much the script slows your aim.

ADS - ADS Dampening (Range: 10 - 100, Default: 60)
   How much your aim slows down when you hold L2 (aim down sights).
   Lower = MORE slowdown = very sticky aim, crosshair barely moves.
   Higher = LESS slowdown = fast aim, almost no stickiness.
   IF YOU FEEL STUCK IN MUD: Raise this to 70-80.
   IF YOU OVERSHOOT TARGETS: Lower this to 45-55.

HIP - Hip Fire Speed (Range: 30 - 100, Default: 75)
   Sensitivity when NOT aiming (hip fire / running around).
   Lower = slower hip fire aim = more control in close fights.
   Higher = faster hip fire = quicker 180 turns.
   MOST PLAYERS: Leave at 70-80.

TRN - Turn Assist (Range: 50 - 100, Default: 85)
   How fast you can turn around when making big stick movements.
   50 = Heavy turn dampening (slow 180s but very controlled)
   100 = No dampening (full speed turns, maximum responsiveness)
   LOWER for more control, HIGHER for faster reactions.

=== PAGE 4: SYSTEM ===
Controller and system settings. Only change these if needed.

DZN - Deadzone (Range: 5 - 25, Default: 13)
   Must match your in-game controller deadzone setting.
   Your Fortnite deadzone 0.05 = set this to 5
   Your Fortnite deadzone 0.10 = set this to 10
   Your Fortnite deadzone 0.13 = set this to 13
   WRONG VALUE = stick drift or weak aim assist.

DZR - Right Stick Deadzone (Range: 3 - 15, Default: 8)
   Deadzone for your RIGHT stick (aim stick). Works the same as DZN but
   only affects aiming, not movement.
   Must match your in-game right stick deadzone setting.
   Your Fortnite right deadzone 0.05 = set this to 5
   Your Fortnite right deadzone 0.08 = set this to 8
   WRONG VALUE = aim drift or sluggish aiming.

TFD - Tap Fire Delay (Range: 10 - 60, Default: 30)
   Time between automatic tap-fire shots, in tenths of a second.
   10 = 1 second between shots (fast tap fire)
   30 = 3 seconds between shots (default, controlled pace)
   60 = 6 seconds between shots (very slow, deliberate)
   LOWER for faster semi-auto fire (shotgun spam).
   HIGHER for slow precision weapons (sniper, bow).

=== PAGE 5: TRACK ===
These settings boost aim assist while you are SHOOTING in ADS. They make
the crosshair follow enemies more on its own. Default is 0 (off) so
your current aim feel is unchanged until you turn these on.

TRK - Track Boost (Range: 0 - 30, Default: 0)
   Increases the rotation circle size while you are firing in ADS.
   This triggers Fortnite's rotational aim assist harder, making the
   crosshair pull towards enemies more aggressively while shooting.
   0  = Off (normal rotation, same as before)
   10 = Mild boost (slightly more pull while shooting)
   20 = Strong boost (noticeable tracking improvement)
   30 = Maximum (very aggressive pull, may feel unnatural)
   START WITH 10-15 and go up from there.

STK - Stick Boost (Range: 0 - 40, Default: 0)
   Extra slowdown applied to your aim ONLY while shooting in ADS.
   This makes your crosshair heavier/slower while firing, giving
   Fortnite's aim assist more control to keep you on target.
   0  = Off (no extra slowdown while shooting)
   10 = Light slowdown (a bit stickier during fire)
   20 = Medium (crosshair noticeably heavier while shooting)
   40 = Maximum (very slow aim while firing, maximum stick)
   WORKS BEST combined with TRK for full auto-tracking feel.
   WARNING: Too high makes it hard to switch targets mid-fight.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   PART 4: QUICK TUNING TIPS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

FOR STRONGER AIM ASSIST:
   Raise ROT to 25-30 and MAG to 10-15.
   Lower ADS to 45-50 for more stickiness.

FOR MAXIMUM AUTO-TRACKING (crosshair follows enemy):
   Set TRK to 15-20 and STK to 15-20.
   Lower ADS to 50 for extra stickiness.
   This gives the strongest "aimbot feel" within Fortnite's system.

FOR MORE NATURAL / SUBTLE FEEL:
   Keep ROT at 15-20 and MAG at 5-7.
   Keep TRK and STK at 0.
   Keep ADS at 60-70.

FOR CLOSE-RANGE FIGHTS (Shotgun/SMG):
   Lower MSP to 8-10 for faster snap.
   Lower HIP to 50-60 for more control.
   Set TRK to 10-15 for stronger tracking up close.

FOR LONG-RANGE (AR/Sniper):
   Raise MSP to 20-25 for smoother tracking.
   Lower ADS to 40-50 for maximum stickiness at range.
   Set STK to 15-20 for steady aim while shooting.

IF YOUR CHARACTER WALKS BY ITSELF (Stick Drift):
   Raise DZN by 2-3 points.
   Raise DZR by 1-2 points.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   PART 5: HOW TO RESET TO DEFAULTS
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

OPTION 1 (EASY): Open the menu (L2 + OPTIONS), then press TRIANGLE.
   All settings instantly reset to defaults. The screen shows "RESET OK!"
   and the controller vibrates to confirm. Settings are saved automatically.

OPTION 2: Re-flash the script to your Cronus Zen.
   All saved settings will be cleared and defaults will load.

Default values: ROT 19, HLX 12, SCN 2, MAG 7, MSP 15,
ADS 60, HIP 75, TRN 85, DZN 13, DZR 8, TFD 30, TRK 0, STK 0.
